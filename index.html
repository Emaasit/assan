The Problem

Slow adoption of Data Science in Developing Countries

The developing part of the world is still lagging behind in the adoption of Data Science, especially for social good.

NGOs face a multitude of Questions about Data

NGOs are faced with a multitude of questions on how to best collect, analyze and visualize data so as to find hidden and actionable insights that will guide their decision making processes.

NGOs face Skepticism

NGOs are faced with some skepticism because many cannot demonstrate their impact to society.
