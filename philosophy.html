    • The Company

      SparkIQ Labs™ is a non-profit organisation that uses Data Science for Social Good in developing countries. Our objective is to bring high-impact organizations dedicated to solving the world’s biggest challenges together with leading data scientists to improve the quality of, access to and understanding of data in the social sector.

      Get in Touch

      If you are are an NGO that needs to make sense of your data, find hidden and actionable insights or show your impact to society, we encourage you to contact us.

      If you are are a data scientist , analyst or enthusiast who is curious about using your skills for social good and make the developing world a better place, we encourage you to contact us.
